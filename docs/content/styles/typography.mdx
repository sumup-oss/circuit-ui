## Font weights

Circuit has two font weights, regular and bold. You can only toggle the
boldness of `Text`, but not `Heading` or `Subheading`.

<Type size={Text.MEGA} bold component={Text} fontWeight="bold" name="text"/>
<Type size={Text.MEGA} component={Text} name="text" fontWeight="regular"/>

## Best practices

- Do not directly use `theme.typography` settings, but rather use the
  specialized components `Heading`, `Subheading`, and `Text`.
