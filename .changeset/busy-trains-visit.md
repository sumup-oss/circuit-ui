Added spacing utility classes to `utilClasses` based on the spacing scale. They are available for all spacing sizes (`bit`, `byte`, `kilo`, `mega`, `giga`, `tera`, `peta`, `exa`, and `zetta`) and can be applied either uniformly (`.marginSize`) or directionally (`.marginDirectionSize`).
